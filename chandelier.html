Under the Light

Maya, 2020

A chandelier I created when thinking about my dream victorian house. While doing some dream apartment search in London, I saw that many of the flats had chandeliers in their rooms. I thought what if I had one in my dream house. I created this chandelier based off of references of chandeliers on google search. I created a candle shaped lightbulb as I would not want to have to constantly light the candles in my house.
