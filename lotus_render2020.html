Nymphéas

Maya, 2020

An image I created out of my imagination of a waterlily pond.

As the new year had just started, fresh from winter break and fun adventures, I created this lily pond. I was really proud of it as it was the first thing I made that I truly was about to make without a reference next to me. I was glad with how it turned out and it was the first piece of art I made that a friend wanted a copy of.

I really love creating water lilies as it is my Chinese name my grandmother gave me.

This is where I was trying to experiment with the amount of petal levels I wanted to put in the different flowers. I wanted them to be similar and unified.
