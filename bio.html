Lauren Chun





Lauren Chun is an interactive digital media artist and creator. Lauren is a Bay Area based digital media artist working out of San Jose, CA. She is currently pursuing her BFA in Digital Media Arts at San Jose State University. Lauren is best known for her 3D sculptures exploring themes of self-expression and prop making. Using digital means to create her work, it is seen to blur the lines between the digital and physical. Through her work, she spearheads collaborative initiates with other artists to promote empowerment and self-action in others.
