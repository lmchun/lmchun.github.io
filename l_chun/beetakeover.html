Year Bee

Found Objects and 3D printing, 2018

In the year 2030, 200 super honey bees broke out of a United States government lab bent on saving the bees. These bees turned on us and we all died leaving the world to the bees, now the bees are making a home of our materials. In order to adapt to the pollution of plastic the bees body mutated. The mutation allows them to consume plastic waste as well as produce their own plastic substance which they use to make new hives.

Along side Kira Boyer, Kevin Nguyen, Kong Vue, Sharonfaye Decastro worked on the other pieces. I made the honeycomb on the portable tv.
