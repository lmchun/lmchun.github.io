Little Bites of Memories

Maya, 2020

Here I made a 3D model of dim sum, Cantonese style foods served in little steam baskets and dishes usually steamed. This was an important render to me as in the times of 2020, I miss all experiences that come with eating good food with my family. It was one of my family meal to share when sharing my culture with friends.
