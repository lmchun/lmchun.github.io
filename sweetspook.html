Spooky Sweet Treat

3D Printing, Chocolate, 2019

During Halloween, the CADRE Student Organization holds a halloween festivity. In 2019, it was hosted on first friday Nov 1st at the Hammer Theater.

For the event, I teamed up with Anna Wong to create chocolates to enjoy. I chose to make jack-o-bears, modeled in maya and 3D printed. The print was then submerged into silicone to create a mold to pour chocolate into.
