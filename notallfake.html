It's not All Fake

3D printing, 2018

In society, we take for granted that there is surveillance all around us. We chose to subject ourselves to surveillance to be able to be an active part of society via social media site Snapchat, instagram, and tik tok, to means of everyday like with google's multiple products we use on an everyday basis. Everything is curated for us due to the constant surveillance companies use to cater to the product, being you. There is a price to free access and nothing is off limits.

The subject of surveillance is seen through the surveillance camera but this is only a representation of the physical surveillance that is present. Digital surveillance happens without a phsyical representation, it leaves no trace.

This piece was shown in the group show Peep Show, displayed in October 2018, at the Social Policy, Downtown San Jose.
